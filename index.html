About 100 Shapes

100 Shapes are a user experience (UX) & digital design studio based in London UK. We specialise in the design, prototyping and development of bespoke enterprise business systems; creating internal business tools to help large organisation run more productively.

We help identify inefficient workflows and processes within large organisations and design systems and tools to change them for the better. We do this by putting staff at the centre of the design process because we understand that happier employees and better designed business tools mean more efficient businesses.
