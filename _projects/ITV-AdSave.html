ITV’s commercial department wanted to make marketing messages within its online TV service more relevant and useful. In response, 100 Shapes developed Ad Save, a tool that allowed viewers to save and collect offers to a ‘wallet’ within ITV Player, and redeem them across all devices.

We designed and built a working prototype and tested it in a live pilot within the real ITV Player interface. Targeted audiences were genuinely receptive to the personalised wallet experience and the perceived increase in relevance of the interactive ads.

Following successful usability testing we held a presentation and future-applications workshop with senior ITV stakeholders, demonstrating a range of use cases for interactive ads and how they could form a key element of ITV’s future customer engagement strategy.
