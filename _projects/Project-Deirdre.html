Named for ITV’s beloved Deirdre Barlow, Project Deidre centralised and simplified ITV’s complex programme promotion system

Our brief was to review review and fix a convoluted, time consuming production and approval process between creative, marketing, scheduling and legal teams across multiple simultaneous campaigns.

An embedded 100 Shapes team worked closely with ITV to design a simple, accessible promo database. By creating a ‘single source of truth’ with an integrated push email system, we were able to dramatically simplify workflows and reduce user clicks, double-ups and error rates.

Deirdre increased efficency so markedly that ITV no longer needed to emplo additional staff in busy seasons, delivering substantial cost savings to the business. The system won ITV’s 2016 Technology Supplier for Innovation and Design Award.
