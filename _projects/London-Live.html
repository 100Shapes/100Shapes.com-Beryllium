London Live was a new local TV channel backed by the Evening Standard and Independent newspapers. Our brief was to translate its complete content offering – news, weather, entertainment, sport, programme listings, social media, live and on-demand TV – to the web.

100 Shapes worked closely with sample audiences to research genuine user consumption behaviours and expectations. We adapted the already-developed broadcast brand for online use and developed a flexible, future-safe design across desktop, mobile and responsive formats. We also built a new tool for curating and moderating ‘hyperlocal’ Twitter streams – the basis for our later Climb.social tool – and delivered the whole new digital platform right on time for launch.
